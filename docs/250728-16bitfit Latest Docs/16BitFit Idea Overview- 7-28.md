In order to have their avatar grow and advance, the user will log their workouts and food intake. When they log healthy foods and log workouts, then the stats of the avatar go up. If they log unhealthy foods, skip workouts, or donâ€™t log at all, the avatars stats slowly go down and when it reaches a critical low point in their stats, the avatar changes to an avatar that looks sick/tired/sluggish. After 10 days of logged workouts, the avatar evolves to level 2\. After 30 days of logged workouts, they evolve to level 3\. After 50 days of logged workouts, they evolve to level 4\.
